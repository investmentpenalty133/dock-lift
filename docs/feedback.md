Feedback & Support
===

DockLift helps your Dock bring windows to the Space and display you are actually using. Your feedback makes the product better.

If you run into issues (for example a specific app that will not move, multi-display edge cases, or Accessibility authorization problems), have questions, or want to suggest features, please reach out. Reports with **macOS version**, **app name**, and a short description of the Dock / Space / display setup are especially helpful.

You can contact us through the app or by emailing **kennyiseeyou@gmail.com**.
