Privacy Policy
===

Thank you for using **DockLift**. We take privacy seriously. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information Collection

DockLift is a **local** macOS utility. It does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal user data to our servers or third-party analytics services for product tracking.

### a. Local data

- Preferences (enable/disable, Space/display options, ignore list, launch at login, etc.) are stored **locally** via system user defaults on your Mac.  
- We do not upload these settings to remote servers.

### b. Accessibility

- DockLift requires **Accessibility** permission to list windows of other apps and to raise or move them.  
- Window titles, positions, and related UI metadata may be read **in memory on your device** solely to provide the lift feature.  
- This information is not sent to remote servers by DockLift.

### c. Log data

- DockLift does not operate a cloud logging backend for your usage.  
- Any diagnostic messages stay on your machine (e.g. local console during development).

## Use of Data

Local preferences and Accessibility-derived window information are used only to:

- Decide when to lift a window  
- Move or activate windows according to your settings  
- Remember your ignore list and options  

## Third-Party Components

DockLift may use system frameworks and local libraries that run on your Mac. They do not change the fact that DockLift itself does not sell or share your personal data for advertising.

## Data Security

Because DockLift does not transmit your window content or preferences to our servers, primary protection is your Mac’s own security (user account, FileVault, system updates). Please keep Accessibility access limited to apps you trust.

## User Control

- You can change or delete DockLift preferences at any time in Settings or by removing the app’s preferences.  
- You can revoke Accessibility access in **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility**.  
- Uninstalling the app removes the application; local preference files may remain until cleared by the system or you.

## Changes

We may update this policy when the product changes. Material updates may be noted in the app website or release notes.
